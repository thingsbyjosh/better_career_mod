# Better Career Mod (BCM)

An open-world career overhaul for [BeamNG.drive](https://www.beamng.com/) — start in an empty garage and progress realistically through a living economy.

## Features

- **Banking & Credit** — Multi-account system with FICO-inspired credit scoring
- **Vehicle Marketplace** — 50+ curated listings with 9 seller archetypes and realistic negotiation
- **10 Brand Dealerships** — Each with their own website, catalog, and purchase flow
- **PlanEx Courier** — 20-level delivery dispatch with loaner vehicles, route optimization, and special routes
- **Police & Pursuits** — 3-level escalation with interceptors, spike strips, and persistent heat
- **O'Really Auto Parts** — Browse, preview, and purchase parts with 3 delivery options
- **Paint Shop** — 3-tier garage system with per-part painting and custom finishes
- **Dynamic Weather** — Seasonal weather with road surface effects and traffic impact
- **Loans & Mortgages** — 4 lenders, auto-payments, repossession system
- **Real Estate** — Property purchasing with taxes and garage tiers
- **Windows XP Desktop** — IE6 browser with 14+ themed websites
- **iPhone-style Phone** — 15+ apps for banking, delivery, marketplace, and more
- **Bilingual** — Full English and Spanish support

## Installation

1. Download the latest release from [Releases](https://github.com/thingsbyjosh/better_career_mod/releases)
2. Place `better_career_mod.zip` in `%LOCALAPPDATA%\BeamNG.drive\current\mods\`
3. Launch BeamNG.drive — look for the BCM icon in the main menu

## Requirements

- BeamNG.drive 0.38+
- West Coast USA map (primary), Italy (in development)
- Not compatible with other career overhaul mods
