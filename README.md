# Better Career Mod (BCM)

A career overhaul for [BeamNG.drive](https://www.beamng.com/) that rethinks the vanilla experience as a grounded, living world. You start with your grandfather's beat-up Miramar — no money, no handouts. A phone, a computer, and a map full of things you can't afford yet.

**Current version: v0.5.0-preview3** — Early beta. Things can break. Feedback welcome.

## What's in it

### Multi-map travel *(new in v0.5)*
Drive up to a travel node, open the world atlas, pick a destination, pay the toll. Your vehicle, full trailer train, cargo, and per-map heat all come with you. Fog-of-war discovery — you can only travel to maps you've visited or their direct neighbors.

### Per-map garages & rentals *(new in v0.5)*
When you land on a new map you need somewhere for the car. Rent by the day, buy outright through Belasco Realty, or use the free backup garage on each map (limited to 2 vehicles, repair-only).

### PlanEx Delivery
A proper courier career. Drive to a depot, load your vehicle, deliver across multiple stops, bring returns back. 37 pack types across 5 tiers with stacking risk mechanics — fragile cargo, temperature timers, urgent deliveries, speed limits. 5-star rating per delivery, 20-level progression from temp driver to fleet captain. Six loaner vehicle tiers with daily rental fees.

### eBoy Motors — the used car market
Every listing is procedurally unique. 9 seller archetypes (grandmas who underprice, flippers who know the market, scammers with rolled-back odometers, dealer pros who expect you to haggle). Chat-based negotiation with mood systems. Test drives, paid inspectors, hidden defects that surface days after purchase. List your own cars and negotiate from the other side.

### Windows XP Desktop
An actual desktop with a working IE-style browser — tabs, address bar, loading animations. Every system in the mod lives inside that browser: bank, dealerships, marketplace, PlanEx dispatch, parts shop, real estate, email, weather, news. Plus a paint shop, dynamometer, parts installer terminal, and garage manager.

### iPhone with 19 apps
Banking, PlanEx dispatch, marketplace, iMessage for NPC conversations, weather, calendar, contacts, wallet, heat tracker, credit score, loans, parts tracking, settings. Auto-closes while driving fast.

### Parts — O'Really Auto Parts
Buying a part isn't instant. Browse the catalog, add to cart, choose warehouse pickup or paid delivery. Domestic and imported parts ship from different facilities. Install them yourself via the FIXD Pro terminal (with cascade protection) or pay a shop.

### Adaptive police
Drive normally and you barely see them. NPCs report reckless driving. Pursuits escalate by damage, not timers — cause enough and interceptors come at you head-on. Persistent per-map heat that decays over real time. Police recognize your car at high heat. Country-aware pools — Italy gets Carabinieri, USA gets American cruisers. Mobile radars, fixed cameras, spike strips, tiered fines.

### Paint shop
Per-part painting. Hood one color, fenders another, roof something else. Basic palette from the start; professional finishes (metallic, matte, chrome, pearlescent) unlock as you upgrade your garage.

### Supported maps
- **West Coast USA** — the original, fully populated
- **Italy** — fully populated since v0.5
- **East Coast USA** — drivable, travel-accessible, dedicated content coming
- **Utah** — drivable, travel-accessible, dedicated content coming

### Also in the mod
- FICO-style credit score, 4 lenders, weekly loan payments, mortgages, repossession
- Real estate with purchase and rental tracking
- Dynamic weather — 6 states, seasonal probabilities, smooth transitions, affects grip and traffic
- Full 365-day calendar with real seasons, sleep system, configurable time speed
- Dynamometer with HP/torque curves
- Fully bilingual — English and Spanish

## Installation

1. Download the latest release from [Releases](https://github.com/thingsbyjosh/better_career_mod/releases)
2. Place `better_career_mod.zip` in `%LOCALAPPDATA%\BeamNG.drive\current\mods\` — **don't unzip it**, BeamNG reads zips directly
3. Launch BeamNG.drive and start a **new career** (existing vanilla saves won't work with BCM)

**Requires BeamNG.drive v0.34+.** Not compatible with other career overhaul mods.
